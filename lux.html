Lux

LUX was my senior university project. The game is a mobile dungeon crawler in which the player must utilize touch controls to navigate obstacles, traps, and enemies while trying to clear increasingly difficult maze-like levels.
The player assumes the role of a ball of light and your light aura is representative of your health. Taking damage diminishes your light (health) which also reduces your view radius in the dark. While your light does regenerate over time it is critical to assemble the 3 energy orbs on each map before you become overwhelmed by enemies and obstacles.
