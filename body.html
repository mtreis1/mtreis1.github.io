Gomi Gomi Dash

The Body Systems is a concept from my senior year in University. This touch kiosk application is designed to teach human anatomy through interaction with segments of the human body.
The application works by letting the user touch different regions of the body on the right side to display corresponding information on the left. The ability to magnify sections of muscle and bone as well as reveal the structural layers of anatomy encourages deeper exploration and learning than other forms of passive engagement.
