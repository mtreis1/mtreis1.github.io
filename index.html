Hello! I'm a developer from Saint Louis Missouri who specializes in Game Design.

I graduated with a Bachelors in Interactive Design from Maryville Univeristy in the spring of 2018 and have been working as an App and Game Devloper ever since. The following is a sampling of some of university and professional work.

VIRTUAL ROOT

Augmented reality application developed for Monsanto that showcases various crops and the threats they may face.

SHARE THE SMILES

Augmented reality project in which toddlers and parents can interact and take photos with their favorite disney characters by scanning products.

SOLAR SYSTEM AR TECH DEMO

A tech demo I developed to showcase our solar system in AR space on IOS.

ROLLERCOASTER TYCOON STORY

Puzzle and park building mobile game developed at Graphite Lab in collaboration with Atari.

FIND US

Card matching game developed for US SOY to teach and test people on their knowledge of products using soy.

LUX

Action platforming mobile game designed for my senior year capstone project at Maryville University.

GOMI GOMI DASH

Internship game project for Graphite Lab in which a rolling raccoon must collect all the trash and beautify the park.

BODY SYSTEMS

Concept for an interactive kiosk for the Saint Louis Science Center intended to teach human anatomy and physiology.
