Hello! I'm Michael Treis.
I'm a designer & developer from Tennessee who works on Game Design.

I graduated with a Bachelors in Interactive Design from Maryville Univeristy in the spring of 2018 and have been working as an App and Game Devloper ever since. The following is a sampling of some of my professional work.

ROLLERCOASTER TYCOON STORY

Puzzle and park building mobile game developed at Graphite Lab in collaboration with Atari.

CLUE

The classic board game digitally reimagined for the Infinity Game Table.

GENSMAK TRIVIA

Gensmak is a pop culture trivia game with different questions for every generation in your family.

VIRTUAL ROOT

Augmented reality application developed for Monsanto that showcases various crops and the threats they may face.

Verizon Trivia

A web based trivia app developed for the Drive In that lets movie-goers compete for prizes from their phones.

SHARE THE SMILES

Augmented reality project in which toddlers and parents can interact and take photos with their favorite disney characters by scanning products.

Lamaze Play

A free, science-based resource designed to help you learn how to use Lamaze toys to play with your baby using the developmental features of each toy.

Doodle Bear Studio

An app that allows kids to scan, decorate, play, and record with their favorite doodle bears.
